## Questions & Answers

**1. How do you balance reactivity with following the roadmap?**

* **Answer:** I use a "buffer" system in sprints for urgent tasks. I prioritize by impact: if a request blocks the final goal, we adapt; if not, it goes to the next sprint to protect the roadmap's integrity.

**2. How do you handle conflict within a team?**

* **Answer:** I address it immediately through individual 1-on-1s to understand each perspective. I focus on technical facts and project goals rather than personalities to find a neutral, constructive compromise.

**3. In an AI project, what are the priorities at the start, middle, and end?**

* **Answer:**
* **Start:** Data quality and defining clear KPIs with the business teams.
* **Middle:** Model performance, robust validation, and iterative feedback.
* **End:** Deployment, monitoring (drift detection), and user adoption.

### Trap question :

Q : "Fabien, we just saw a demo of a new Generative AI feature from a competitor. We need to pivot and integrate this into our current solution immediately. Can we add this to the next sprint and have it ready by next month? We can't afford to fall behind."

R : "I understand the competitive pressure, but I cannot commit to this immediately without a proper assessment / specification / value study. As a Tech Lead, my priority is to deliver a reliable solution, not a rushed prototype."
